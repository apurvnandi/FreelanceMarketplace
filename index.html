How it works

A clean marketplace flow from project post to payment.

01

Client posts a project

Project title, budget, and requirements are stored in MySQL and shown to freelancers.

02

Freelancers apply

Freelancers submit proposals and can track their pending or accepted applications.

03

Client hires and pays

The selected freelancer is assigned, payment is recorded, and earnings update automatically.
